Hackathon

About | Rules | Contacts

"Programming today is a race between software engineers striving to build bigger and better idiot-proof programs, and the universe trying to produce bigger and better idiots. So far, the universe is winning"

-Rick Cook

About

Let your imagination flow free and enable you to push the boundaries of what can be done. Whack your brain in this 24 hour hackathon where optimization, skill, proficiency, hard work and trying till the very end matters! Leave no excuse to not build something that doesn’t blow our minds!

Rules

  • Phases

    1. Registration and announcement of the theme.

    2. Build a working app on platform you wish in 24 hours.

    3. At the end, give a small presentation in front of experts.

    4. Winners will be awarded with prizes.

  • Rules

    The link for the contest is Hack2connect
    • There can be a maximum of 3 members in a team.
    • All the code, design, must be created/developed during the Hackathon only, so that all the participants start off on the same footing.
    • Internet and Wifi connectivity will be provided throughout the event.
    • To participate in the Hackathon, each member of the team, must register for the Hackathon online through the website.
    • The final product should be deployed as a mobile app, web app, software or any other platform accessible to the user.
    • Participants should bring their own resources. (Laptops, chargers etc.) .
    • It is mandatory for the participants to bring their personal identity proof.
    • Theme for event will be provided on the time of the Hackathon itself only.
    • Any member of the team can take break for refreshments at any time.
    • No type of PLAGIARISM would be entertained. Any team found doing so will be disqualified straight away.
    • In case of any dispute, the decision taken by event heads will be final.

  • Prize money worth Rs. 60,000/-

  • Registartion Fees: Rs. 600/- (Per team)

About

The perfect marathon for your grey cells – Techathlon, brings to you three exciting, tense and enthralling events, each of whose score will add up to determine who the real conqueror is!

Rules

  • Round A: Mathsmania

    When mathematicians and computer scientists unite, the results are beyond your imagination. Welcome to a mathematical puzzled styled contest that challenges you to untangle the intricate enigmas of mathematics.

  • Round B : Awkward Code

    Debug your way through the lines of code and predict the output as participants rush to battle it out for Awkward Code, the ultimate test of debugging and finding outputs!

  • Round C :Algo Designing

    Have you got the nerves to solve some very intricate system design problems? Prepare your bits of paper and pen and get ready to show the world some of the best algorithms one can come up with in Algo Designing!

  • 1. The event will have three rounds. Each carrying equal marks. At last the score of the team is added in all three. The one having maximum points is declared the winner.

  • 2. This is only onsite competition.

  • 3. The results would be judged by our expert panel. And their decision is non-negotiable.

  • 4. This is a team competition, participants must participate in teams of at least 2 and at max 3.
